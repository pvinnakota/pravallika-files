A lot of events happne in our Solar system and because of their magnitude and scale, they are known as Atronomical Events.

F

Full Moon

The full Moon is observed from the Earth.

N

New Moon

The Moon is non observable from the Earth.

S

Solar Eclipse

The Moon blocks the Sun when observed from Earth.

L

Lunar Eclipse

The Earth Blocks the Sun rays from reaching the Moon.
